# The Sonnet Prophecy
A warrior in armor bright, \
Leading the forces of light. \
Though but a halfling thing, \
Peace to all he may bring, \
A war started, a war fought, \
To a flag, he ties his knot. \
A signal to his forces there, \
A mighty cross, his to bear. \
In the fray a team arises, \
Together they reap the prizes, \
A warning too, we must give, \
Not all of us will still live. \
A sacrifice must be made. \
Or forever, we dwell in Shade.
