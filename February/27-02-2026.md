# Silent Love
I love a girl \
That doesn't speak \
In common ways.

Hummed melodies, \
Of fav'rite songs,\
Warm our home 

A gentle smile, \
After long day's work \
Keeps me going.

Time alone, \
When the heart hurts, \
Followed by hugs.

Because to me, \
Silent actions, \
Mean more than words.
