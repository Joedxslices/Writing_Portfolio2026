# Quiet Connection
Throughout this month, I've been writing about connection. I did sort of fall off, and took a week's hiatus, give or take. But I always wrote the poems, since I connect with poetry. 

The theme in general of the poems is quiet connection. The quiet ways in which people connect, or don't. Connections are fostered in the quiet. You don't need sound to know there's a connection between two people. Sometimes, it's just felt. I feel like I long for the loud connections, and take somewhat for granted the quiet connections I have. The connections that don't require words are the ones that are often most beneficial. I rarely talk to one of my good friends, but we have a stream of consciousness that connects us as we share memes. We don't need words, necessarily. We need that fundamental spark of understanding.
