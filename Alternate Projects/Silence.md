# What Silence Means
You might take my silence \
To mean disinterest. \
If only you knew...

Silence is my strength, \
My super power. \
My refuge when scared.

Silence sings several songs, \
Melodies patient ears hear, \
Tender songs of the heart.

Silent actions, unnoticed, \
Are the nectar of loving souls \
Who have lost their voices.

Silent eyes, lingering, \
A moment too long, \
Hoping silent eyes to meet.

A silent soul, \
Hiding in plain sight, \
Gathering silent might.

A silent soul, \
Alone with thoughts, \
Never proclaimed.

My silence means, \
I'll never shoot my shot. \
If only you knew.
