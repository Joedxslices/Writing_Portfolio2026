**Prompt:** List Things You Know Now.
# 10 Things I Know Now
1. I am strong and capable.
2. I have a unique way and skill of communicating with people.
3. I want a career in the publishing sphere, preferably as an editor/proofreader (more on the proofreading side.)
4. I have better success in maintaining relationships if I get set up.
5. I enjoy talking about complex subjects more than engaging in small talk.
6. Lemonade is my favorite drink.
7. I enjoy reading cozy mysteries.
8. I excel at ideating, but have trouble with follow through.
9. I get distracted remarkably easily.
10. I can be consistent, while also having bad days.
