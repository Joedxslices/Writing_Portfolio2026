**Prompt:** Numbers matter

# 1 4 3
One, four, three \
Simply means \
You matter to me

Twenty-four seven \
Three sixty-five \
Every day that you're alive

One, four, three \
I love you, \
And you love me

Twenty-four seven \
Three sixty-five \
Grateful you're alive

One, four, three \
Forever in love, \
Just you and me.
